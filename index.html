Upercase Letters

What are uppercase letters?

Capital letters are also called uppercase letters or simply capitals.

For example: A,B,C,D, and so on.

Lowercase Letters

What are lowercase letters?

Lowercase letters are the shorter, smaller versions of letters (like w), as opposed to the bigger, taller versions (like W), which are called uppercase letters or capital letters.

For example: a, b, c, d, and so on

English Uper and Lowercase Letters

What are English Uper and Lowercase Letters?

English Uper and Lowercase Letters are both Uper and lowercase letters.

For example, Aa, Bb, Cc, Dd, and so on.

Whole Numbers

What are whole numbers?

Whole Numbers are those counting positive numbers that start from 0 and go on till infinity.

For example, 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, and so on.
